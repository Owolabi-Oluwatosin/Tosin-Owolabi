About

From the beginning of my tech journey, I have been driven by passion and determination. I started freelancing on platforms like Fiverr, where a $40 UI/UX design project for Esdiac Global System Inc. became my breakthrough. After exceeding expectations, Esdiac returned with another project, and I showcased my skills in frontend development using React.js. Impressed, they entrusted me with larger projects, marking the start of my remote work with them.

On October 15, 2020, I transitioned from Fiverr to working directly with Esdiac. Over time, I evolved from a UI/UX designer to a full-stack developer, mastering technologies like React.js, React Native, Kotlin (KMP), Next.js, Redux, Node.js/Express.js, Nest.js, and DevOps. I contributed to over ten major projects, including Esdiac Video Conference, Esdiac Pay, and Esdiac Coin.

Collaborating with the CTO and team at Esdiac has been instrumental in shaping my technical expertise and leadership skills. This experience, along with other projects, has enriched my career and strengthened my commitment to continuous learning and innovation.

My journey reflects a relentless pursuit of growth and a passion for creating efficient, visually appealing solutions. I’m eager to collaborate with professionals, explore new opportunities, and contribute to impactful projects. Let’s connect and turn your vision into reality!
